# Online and Mobile Banking

## Online Banking

Online banking refers to the ability to access and manage one's bank accounts through the internet and a web browser. This service allows customers to monitor their account balances, transfer funds between accounts, pay bills, and view account statements without having to visit a physical bank branch. Online banking provides customers with the convenience of 24/7 access to their financial information and the ability to conduct transactions from anywhere with an internet connection. This reduces the need for frequent visits to bank branches, saving customers time and effort.

## Mobile Banking

Mobile banking is an extension of online banking that enables customers to access and manage their bank accounts using a mobile application on a smartphone or tablet. Similar to online banking, mobile banking allows customers to check account balances, transfer funds, pay bills, and even deposit checks remotely by taking a picture of the check using the device's camera. The primary benefit of mobile banking is the added convenience and accessibility it provides, allowing customers to conduct banking activities on the go, regardless of their location.

## Benefits of Online and Mobile Banking

Both online and mobile banking have become increasingly popular due to their convenience and the growing trend of digitalization in the financial services industry. These services not only save customers time and effort but also provide a secure and efficient way to manage their finances from the comfort of their homes or while on the move.
